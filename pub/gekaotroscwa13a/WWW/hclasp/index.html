Overview

hclasp is an extension of clasp providing a general declarative framework for incorporating domain-specific heuristics into ASP solving. This is accomplished by extending the first-order modeling language of ASP by a distinguished heuristic predicate. The resulting heuristic information is processed as an equitable part of the logic program and subsequently exploited by the solver when it comes to non-deterministically assigning a truth value to an atom.

hclasp

hclasp incorporates all features of clasp and extends the normal logic program by a distinguished heuristic predicate to express domain-specific heuristic.
